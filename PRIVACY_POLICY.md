# CNS Enterprise API - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** October 26, 2025

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CNS Enterprise API ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our Service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an API account, we collect:
- Company name and contact information
- Billing email address
- Payment information (processed by Stripe)
- API usage preferences and tier selection

### 2.2 API Usage Data
When you use the Service, we automatically collect:
- API requests and responses
- Timestamps and request metadata
- IP addresses and user agents
- Error logs and performance metrics
- Rate limit usage and billing information

### 2.3 User-Submitted Content
Content you submit through API requests, including:
- Messages and conversation text
- User IDs and context data
- Any metadata you include in requests

**Important:** We strongly recommend NOT sending personally identifiable information (PII) or sensitive personal data through the API without proper user consent and data protection measures.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision
We use your information to:
- Process API requests and generate responses
- Authenticate and authorize access
- Monitor service performance and availability
- Enforce rate limits and billing

### 3.2 Billing and Payments
- Calculate usage-based charges
- Process payments through Stripe
- Generate invoices and usage reports
- Detect and prevent fraud

### 3.3 Service Improvement
We may use aggregated, anonymized data to:
- Improve AI model performance
- Optimize service reliability
- Develop new features
- Conduct research and analytics

**We do NOT use your specific API requests to train our models without explicit consent.**

### 3.4 Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to legal requests
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights and safety

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Request/Response Data
- API request and response data is retained for **30 days** for debugging and analytics
- Logs are automatically deleted after 30 days
- You may request earlier deletion at any time

### 4.2 Account Data
- Account information is retained while your account is active
- After account termination, data is deleted within **90 days**
- Billing records are retained for **7 years** for tax and legal compliance

### 4.3 Aggregated Analytics
- Anonymized, aggregated analytics may be retained indefinitely
- This data cannot be used to identify you or your users

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or API data to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 5.2 Service Providers
We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us:
- **Stripe:** Payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy)
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Hosting and data storage (Replit)
- **Analytics Tools:** Service monitoring and error tracking

All service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and data protection requirements.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or in response to:
- Valid legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
- Requests from law enforcement
- Protection of our rights and safety
- Prevention of fraud or security threats

### 5.4 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such change.

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

### 6.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption:** TLS/SSL for data in transit, encryption at rest for sensitive data
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, API key authentication
- **Monitoring:** Real-time security monitoring and anomaly detection
- **Auditing:** Regular security audits and penetration testing

### 6.2 Organizational Safeguards
- Employee training on data protection
- Limited access to production data
- Incident response procedures
- Data breach notification protocols

### 6.3 Your Responsibility
You are responsible for:
- Keeping your API key secure
- Implementing proper access controls in your applications
- Complying with data protection regulations applicable to your use case
- Obtaining necessary user consents before processing personal data

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to:
- Access your account information
- Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format
- Export usage logs and billing data

### 7.2 Correction and Deletion
You may:
- Update your account information at any time
- Request deletion of specific data
- Request account termination and data deletion

### 7.3 Opt-Out Rights
You may opt out of:
- Marketing communications (we don't send marketing emails)
- Analytics and research use of your data (contact us)
- Data retention beyond required legal periods

### 7.4 GDPR Rights (EU Users)
If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
- Right to withdraw consent at any time

### 7.5 CCPA Rights (California Users)
California residents have rights under CCPA:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale (note: we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
